Tim McGraw on Letting the Music Develop

11 May 2015 16h18

Tim McGraw might have a new album out by the end of 2015. Then again, he might not. He wants to, and he’s working on some songs, but in a recent interview with CBS News, he admitted you can’t force it. He said he just started working on the album a couple of weeks ago and is right in the thick of it.

Blake Shelton Serves Up Latest Hit on Hot Country Songs Chart

07 May 2015 19h24

Blake Shelton stirs up his latest hit on Billboard's Hot Country Songs chart (dated May 16), as "Sangria" soars 11-4. The third single from Bringing Back the Sunshine, and his 23rd career top 10, bumps 3-2 on Country Digital Songs. The song vaults after Shelton sang it on the April 27 episode of NBC's The Voice (a performance piggybacked onto his coach's role). Shelton previously performed "Sangria" on the April 19 Academy of Country Music Awards, broadcast on CBS, and which he co-hosted with Luke Bryan.

Lady Antebellum’s Bed Bath & Beyond Heartland Collection Is Hitting the Road

30 April 2015

Lady Antebellum expanded their enterprise to include home linens with their Heartland home collection, which launched at Bed Bath & Beyond this month. The trio, made up of Charles Kelly, Hillary Scott and Dave Haywood, say that the bed and bath lines were created from picking their own favorite items.

Eric Church, Keith Urban Kick Off ACM Awards

19 April 2015, 20h19

The 50th ACM Awards kicked off with two solo male country superstars, co-hosts Luke Bryan and Blake Shelton, introducing two other solo male country superstars, Eric Church and Keith Urban, the latter of whom combined for an energetic joint performance to open the show.

Lady Antebellum's Hillary Scott Fine After Tour Bus Fire

16 April 2015, 00h38

Hillary Scott is letting fans know she's okay after a bus fire in Dallas Thursday morning (April 16).
The local NBC affiliate notes Scott was en route from Nashville to Dallas for the American Country Music Awards at Cowboys Stadium in Arlington.
