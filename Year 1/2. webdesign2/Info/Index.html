Travel

Travel

You can travel by bus, taxi, train, car, motorcylce or bicycle.
If you fly in from another country then you can take a taxi or bus or you can rent a car or bike.

Our festival is situated in South Park (Washington, United States). South Park is a town in the Washington region of United States. South Park airports: The nearest airport is BFI - Seattle Boeing Fld King Co Intl, located 0.9 km north east of South Park.

The Park

Park

On the festival grounds food and drinks are permitted but recycling is mandatory. People who are caught leaving garbage behind, will be fined.

There are multiple EHBO stands all over the festival grounds and there will be medical staff available in the vicinity of the stages.

There is free wifi and safety lockers will be available for the visitors.

Campsite

Campsite

On the campsite you can choose to bring your own tent or you can rent a tent at the check-in desk.

There will be toilets at the campsite, but to use them you have to pay 10 cents.

All weapons and drugs must be handed over at the controle post before entering the festival grounds, where they will be safely stored. You can get them back when you leave the festival.
